Epidemic Models
===============

``FS_GPlib`` implements a broad range of epidemic and diffusion models on static networks.
These models are organized into three sub-categories:

**Compartmental Models.**
Classic compartmental spreading models where nodes transition between discrete states
(e.g., Susceptible, Infected, Recovered) via stochastic contact-based transmission.
This includes SI, SIS, SIR, SEIR, SEIS, SWIR, and their continuous-time variants (SEIR_ct, SEIS_ct).

**Threshold Models.**
Adoption-driven models in which a node becomes active once the fraction of its active neighbors
exceeds a given threshold. This includes the Threshold and KThreshold models.

**Cascade and Profile Models.**
Models capturing one-shot activation cascades or profile-based social contagion,
including Independent Cascades, Profile, and ProfileThreshold.
